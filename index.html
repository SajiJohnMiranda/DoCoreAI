About DoCoreAI

DoCoreAI is an open-source AI toolkit designed to enhance large language model (LLM) interactions through innovative optimization techniques. Combining modern and futuristic design, our project delivers a sleek, minimal interface with powerful, scalable AI solutions.

Features

  • Optimized LLM interactions
  • Advanced intelligence profiling
  • Seamless integration with popular AI frameworks
  • Open source and community driven
